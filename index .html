Why we exist

This isn't about the blunders.

The name's a joke about how everyone starts, hanging pieces, missing forks, resigning drawn positions. Blunderbuss exists so nobody has to learn that alone.

Whether you're an 1800 dropped a pawn on move six or a 400 who just learned how the knight moves, the table has a seat for you. Every review, every study group, every session in voice is free. We just ask that you show up curious.

We run active coaching in both Chess and Shogi, with staff covering everything from total beginners to titled-level prep.

More than just study sessions

There's always something happening.

Learning here isn't just sitting in analysis taking notes. We build real activity around getting better, because chess and shogi stick a lot better when they're actually fun.

🎓

Live Coaching Classes

Coaches host structured classes whenever they're available, real lessons on openings, tactics, and endgames, not just drop-in Q&A.

🎲

Games Built for Learning

We run games and challenges designed to make studying feel like hanging out with friends, learning with people, not just at them.

🏆

Constant Tournaments, Every ELO

Tournaments run all the time and welcome every rating. Compete, have fun, and improve, all in the same game.

🍬

Candy,Our Server Currency

Earn Candy for showing up, learning, and taking part, then spend it around the server. A sweeter way to stay motivated.

✨

And this is just the start. We're constantly expanding and adding new ideas, from new learning tools to brand new concept games on the server. A lot more is coming.
